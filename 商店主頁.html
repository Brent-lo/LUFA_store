🌿 產品理念 / Product Philosophy

我們相信，每一件飾品都值得擁有第二次生命。
透過回收金屬與海廢玻璃，我們將被遺忘的材料轉化為值得珍藏的作品。LÜFA 堅持以手工慢工打造每一件飾品，賦予它獨特的故事與靈魂，也讓佩戴者與地球之間建立一段溫柔的連結。


We believe every piece deserves a second life.
Using recycled metals and ocean-found glass, we transform forgotten materials into meaningful treasures. At LÜFA, every piece is crafted slowly by hand, carrying a story, a soul, and a gentle connection between you and the Earth.

🧵 創始人的故事 / The Founder’s Story

一枚破碎的海玻璃，讓我重新看見「廢棄」的價值。
某次海邊散步時，我撿起一塊磨圓的玻璃碎片。它曾是垃圾，卻因海水的淬鍊，變得溫潤且獨一無二。那一刻，我開始思考：如果被丟棄的材料，也能重生為值得珍藏的飾品呢？
於是，我創立了 LÜFA —— 一個讓時間與雙手賦予材料第二次生命的品牌。


It all began with a single piece of sea glass.
One quiet walk by the shore, I found a rounded shard of glass softened by the ocean. Once discarded, it had been reshaped by nature into something beautiful. That moment changed how I saw waste — not as an end, but as a beginning.
And so I founded LÜFA: a jewelry brand where forgotten materials are given a second life through time, care, and craft.
